The best workplace safety apps for women

Mar 21, 2025 4 min read

In today's modern world, workplace safety is more important than ever before. With the rise of technology, there are now many apps available that can help women stay safe in the workplace. These apps offer a variety of features, from alerting emergency contacts in case of an emergency to providing resources for dealing with harassment or discrimination. In this blog post, we will discuss some of the best workplace safety apps for women.

1. Noonlight

Noonlight is a personal safety app that works by connecting to the user's smartphone. In case of an emergency, the user can simply press a button on the app and their location will be instantly sent to Noonlight's team of certified dispatchers. The dispatcher will then contact emergency services on behalf of the user. This app is especially useful for women who work alone or in remote areas.

2. SafeTrek

SafeTrek is another personal safety app that allows users to alert emergency services with the touch of a button. When the app is activated, the user holds their thumb on the screen. If they release their thumb, SafeTrek will automatically alert emergency services and send them the user's location. This app is ideal for women who feel unsafe walking to their car or taking public transportation at night.

3. bSafe

bSafe is a comprehensive personal safety app that offers features such as GPS tracking, live streaming video, and automatic recording of audio. In case of an emergency, users can simply shake their phone to activate a loud alarm and notify their chosen contacts. bSafe also has a feature called "ThumbSOS" that allows users to discreetly ask for help by pressing the power button. This app is great for women who work in high-risk environments.

4. WorkSafe

WorkSafe is a workplace safety app specifically designed for women. It offers resources and information on how to deal with harassment, discrimination, and other workplace safety issues. WorkSafe also provides tips on how to stay safe in different work environments, such as offices, warehouses, and construction sites. This app is perfect for women who want to take proactive steps to protect themselves at work.

5. Circle of 6

Circle of 6 is a safety app that allows users to choose six trusted contacts to be their "circle." In case of an emergency, users can simply tap the app to send a pre-programmed message to their circle, along with their location. The app also provides resources on how to deal with sexual assault, domestic violence, and other safety concerns. Circle of 6 is a great app for women who want to stay connected with their friends and family while at work.

6. Vigilante

Vigilante is a community-based safety app that alerts users to crimes and emergencies in their area. Users can report incidents and receive real-time alerts from other users. Vigilante also has a feature called "safe walk" that allows users to request a virtual escort when walking alone at night. This app is especially useful for women who work in urban areas or travel frequently for work.

7. My Safetipin

My Safetipin is a safety app that provides users with information on the safety of their surroundings. Users can check the safety score of a location by simply entering the address into the app. My Safetipin also offers tips on how to improve safety in different environments, such as workplaces, parking lots, and public transportation. This app is ideal for women who want to be proactive about their safety while at work.

8. Watch Over Me

Watch Over Me is a personal safety app that allows users to set a timer when they are in an unsafe situation. If the timer expires without being deactivated, the app will automatically alert emergency services and send them the user's location. Watch Over Me also has a feature called "Shake to Alert" that allows users to discreetly ask for help by shaking their phone. This app is perfect for women who work in high-stress environments where they may not always be able to access their phone quickly.

In conclusion, workplace safety is a critical issue for women in today's world. Thankfully, there are many apps available that can help women stay safe while at work. From personal safety apps like Noonlight and SafeTrek to workplace safety apps like WorkSafe and My Safetipin, there are many options for women to choose from. Whether you work in an office, a warehouse, or on the go, these apps can provide you with the peace of mind you need to focus on your work. Stay safe out there!
